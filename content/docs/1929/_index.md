- Richmond named capital: Richmond, Virginia was named the capital of the Confederate States of America on May 8, 1861.
- Camp Jackson Affair: On May 10, 1861, Union military forces clashed with civilians in St. Louis, Missouri, resulting in at least 28 deaths.
